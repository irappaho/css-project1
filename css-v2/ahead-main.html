Typography

Arial Font Family

A contemporary sans serif design, Arial contains more humanist characteristics than many of its predecessors and as such is more in tune with the mood of the last decades of the twentieth century. Easy to read at large and small sizes. For web, we use em units - roughly 18px for body copy. Body and headings are all Arial or a fallback system font like Helvetica.

Heading 1

The <h1> tag is generally used only once on a page, for the page title.

Heading 2

The <h2> is good for sub-headings in content. Sub-headings help users and search engines scan your content and determine what your page is all about.

Heading 3

The <h3> is another useful sub-heading.

Heading 4

You might use an <h4> on occassion.

Heading 5

I rarely if ever use an <h5>. But it is possible.

Heading 6

Never have I ever used an <h6>.
