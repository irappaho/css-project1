AHEAD Iconset

You already have icons that you use for print materials. Rather than relying on what may happen to be available through FontAwesome (which will likely be in use by some of your competitors), we decided to turn existing icons into your own web font.

As a font, you can scale these without loss of quality and style the icons to match print materials more closely. Result: fast loading, crisp-looking icons with a more uniform meaning.
